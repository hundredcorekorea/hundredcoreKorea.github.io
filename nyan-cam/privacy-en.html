Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 18, 2026

NyanCam ("the App") is operated by RuffLabs (Hundred Core) ("the Company"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

DataPurposeRetention
Anonymous User ID (Supabase Anonymous Auth)Focus session records, leaderboardUntil account deletion
Focus session data (start/end time, duration)Statistics, leaderboardUntil account deletion

1.2 Locally Stored Information

  • Settings: Focus time, break time, cat skin, CCTV filter, sound preferences
  • Streak Records: Consecutive focus days, last session date
  • Purchase Status: In-app purchase state (managed by Google Play)

This information is stored only on your device via SharedPreferences and is not transmitted to any server.

1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email, phone number, or other personally identifiable information
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or media
  • Camera or microphone access

2. Data Storage and Security

2.1 Server Storage (Supabase)

  • Focus session records and user statistics are stored on Supabase cloud.
  • We use anonymous authentication ‚Äî no email or password is stored.
  • Data is transmitted via encrypted connections (TLS).

2.2 Data Security

  • Server data is protected by Supabase's Row Level Security policies.
  • On-device data is stored within the Android/iOS security sandbox.
  • Uninstalling the app deletes all on-device data.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

  • The app uses Google AdMob for rewarded advertisements.
  • AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and device information for ad delivery.
  • For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3.2 Google Play In-App Purchases

  • Premium skins and filters are purchased through Google Play's billing system.
  • Payment information is handled directly by Google; the app does not store payment data.

3.3 Supabase

  • Used for anonymous authentication and focus session data storage.
  • For more details, see Supabase Privacy Policy.

4. Notification Permissions

  • The app may request notification permissions for daily streak reminders.
  • This permission is optional ‚Äî the app works fully without it.
  • You can change notification settings at any time in your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

NyanCam is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through in-app notices or store update notes.
