Hundred Core ("we," "us," or "our") operates the following services and applications (collectively, the "Services"):

  • Other mobile applications published under Hundred Core on Google Play and the App Store

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use any of our Services. Please read it carefully. By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and profile picture through Google OAuth or email sign-up.
  • User content: Text inputs, choices, and preferences you provide while using our Services (e.g., tarot questions, TRPG character actions).
  • Payment information: When you make a purchase, payment processing is handled by Lemon Squeezy. We do not store your credit card numbers or banking details.
  • Communications: If you contact us via email, we retain the content of your messages.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, session duration, and interaction patterns.
  • Device data: Device type, operating system, browser type, language settings, and screen resolution.
  • Log data: IP address, access times, and referring URLs.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: We use cookies for authentication, preferences, and analytics. See Section 7 for details.

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google OAuth: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Analytics providers: Aggregated usage statistics from Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Process transactions and send related notifications
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Generate AI-powered content in response to your inputs
  • Personalize your experience and remember your preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our Services
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, and support
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. AI-Generated Content

Our Services use artificial intelligence models provided through OpenRouter (including models from Anthropic, Google, and other providers) to generate content such as tarot readings, fortune interpretations, and TRPG narratives.

  • Your text inputs are sent to AI model providers to generate responses. These inputs are processed according to each provider's data handling policies.
  • AI-generated content is for entertainment purposes only and should not be relied upon as professional advice (medical, financial, legal, or otherwise).
  • We do not use your inputs to train AI models. However, third-party AI providers may have their own data retention policies.
  • All AI-generated content in our Services is clearly marked as such, in compliance with applicable AI disclosure regulations.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at osu355@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
